/ 00 — THE LAB

The distance between an idea and a working product should be short.

One Man Labs is an independent studio built around a single, continuous engineering loop. Product thinking, interface design, electronics, software, infrastructure, and deployment stay at the same desk.

That means fewer translations, faster prototypes, and systems designed as a whole—not a pile of handoffs between disconnected teams.

/ 04 — WHY ONE MAN LABS

The name is literal.
The ambition isn't small.

One Man Labs is the independent product studio of Nick Hughes—a place to build useful, technically ambitious things without separating invention from execution.

A single builder owns the thread from the first sketch through architecture, implementation, testing, launch, and support. When a project needs a specialist, the collaboration stays direct and the accountability stays clear.

The result is a lab that can move from a circuit board to an iPhone interface, from a GPU workload to a deployment pipeline, without losing the original intent along the way.
