01 The product

Component selection is a systems problem.

A part can look correct in isolation and still fail inside a build because of voltage, protocol, connector, weight, capacity, or compliance constraints. Drone Part Picker makes those relationships visible while the system is being assembled.

The prototype combines a searchable component catalog with live build totals, compatibility feedback, product comparison, shareable configurations, and bill-of-material export.

02 Product capabilities
01

Browse the catalog

Search and filter hundreds of parts across category, manufacturer, price, weight, voltage, protocol, and compliance.

02

Check compatibility

Rules evaluate voltage ranges, protocols, connectors, frame capacity, and thrust-to-weight relationships.

03

Build interactively

A familiar category-based workspace tracks selections, cost, weight, issues, and suggestions in real time.

04

Compare parts

Side-by-side product specifications make meaningful differences easier to see before purchase.

05

Share the result

Builds persist locally, travel through shareable URLs, and export as a practical bill of materials.

06

Explain the warning

Compatibility findings are presented as errors, warnings, or suggestions instead of a single opaque score.
